The table has laws

Do not chase a number.
Build a way out.

Choose one to five cards. Turn them between Light and Dark. You only get a few chances to flip or swap cards, so every choice matters.

Every journey brings new fights, strange events, useful shops, powerful rewards, and Keepers who change the rules. Winning comes from reading the table and making the right move.

01

Choose Light or Dark

Keep a card safe in the Light, or turn it Dark for a greater reward with more risk.

02

Every round changes

New rules can help, punish, or completely change the kind of hand you should play.

03

Shape each journey

Find new powers, change your cards, choose your path, and build a different way to win each time.
