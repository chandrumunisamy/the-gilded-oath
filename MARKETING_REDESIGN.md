# Marketing redesign

This version replaces the generic product-page structure with a focused game campaign built around one identity: **the House, the oath, Light and Dark, and the Keepers**.

## Removed
- GitHub/project-folder calls to action
- Device-fit messaging
- Technical download language
- Generic feature boxes
- Awkward automatically wrapped headline phrases
- Scroll listeners and mobile motion effects

## Added
- Controlled two-line campaign headlines
- A clear story from hook → play → journey → enemies → download
- Player-facing language throughout
- Static, exact-aspect-ratio image frames
- Lightweight desktop-only entrance motion
- Direct Android download as the only commercial action
